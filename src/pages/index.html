Our Journey

Prosper emerged out of one designer's response to the human suffering of the refugee crisis in Europe. Since launching in October 2015, a global community of skilled digital workers has centralized around a single mission:

To transform the refugee crisis into an opportunity to foster an integrated society where every person is cherished and prosperity abounds.

Projects

  • Solve a real human problem of the refugee crisis
  • Be sustainable, scalable and significant
  • Solutions must be measurable

Volunteer

As a skilled digital worker, you can make a difference in the lives of refugees by offering what you do best

There are problems of the refugee crisis that only you can solve. The combination of design and technology has placed digital workers in a unique position within the world we live in. If you have skills, passion and experience you can make a significant, scalable and sustainable impact alongside our community.

Whether you're able to offer an hour a week or ten—we make it easy for skilled digital workers to help refugees prosper. As a volunteer you can:

  • Contribute to one of our projects and/or
  • Mentor an apprentice

We have immediate need in the following disciplines:

  • Ethnography & User Research
  • Agile Project Management
  • Human-Centered Design
  • Web Development & Architecture
  • Social Media & Digital Marketing
  • Others*

*If your skillset isn't listed above and you'd like to get involved, please contact us. The need for passionate professionals with relevant skills is immense. We enjoy matching skilled workers with meaningful projects that make a significant impact on helping the refugee crisis.

Prosper Academy

An online apprenticeship for designers and developers to grow their skills through contributing to refugee crisis projects

After launching Prosper, we learned that there's a global community of designers and developers that have enough skills to contribute to a project but lack the experience to launch a meaningful career. Some of the members of this community are citizens, and some are refugees.

Prosper Academy is our response to this community's needs.
