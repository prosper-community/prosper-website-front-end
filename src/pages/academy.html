Prosper Academy

An online apprenticeship for designers and developers to grow their skills through contributing to refugee crisis projects

After launching Prosper, we learned that there's a large community of designers and developers that have enough skills to contribute to a project but lack the experience to launch a meaningful career.

We realized in talking to members of this community that we needed to take a different approach to serving their needs. Rather than prescribe a set curriculum to help people grow, we've chosen to work with each apprentice to define a custom skill plan that reflects their needs and experience. This allows each apprentice to grow in the directtion and manner that suits them best.

With a skill plan in place, we match apprentices to real tasks on Prosper projects. This allows apprentices to learn by doing while also having the freedom to fail and support of an expert mentor.

This structure reflects our belief that people learn best when they:

  • are driven by passion and
  • can collaborate with other professionals on meaningful projects.

our solution

We've designed a custom platform where apprentices (citizens and refugees alike):

  • Work with a career coach to define a custom skill plan
  • Collaborate with an interdisciplinary team on real projects within the refugee crisis
  • Meet weekly with an expert mentor
  • Use design and technology to solve real problems for real people
  • Learn to articulate decisions to the project team
  • Gain relevant experience and contacts to land a dream job

Two Tracks

Because most of our apprentices already have experience in the industry and are looking to gain specialized skills, we've structured our course as two parallel tracks that collaborate with one another on Prosper projects:

Design Track

Work with expert human-centered designers to solve real problems for real people amidst the refugee crisis...

Development Track

Collaborate with experienced developers to craft web-based solutions to problems of the refugee crisis...

open enrollment

If you're interested in growing your skills through contributing to meaningful projects, please submit your details below. We will follow-up with an interview request so that we can begin to craft your custom skill plan based upon your experience, goals, and lifestyle.

We understand that the needs of each apprentice is different, and that's why we take the time to develop a custom plan to meet your needs.

bursary for refugees

If you are a refugee and are interested in joining Prosper Academy but don't have the funds, you can inquire about our bursary by submitting your details below. We've also designed a structured framework to support people from challenging circumstances. We are sensitive to the fact that our bursary applicants may have unique lifestyle obstacles and are prepared to work with you to create the right environment for you to grow.

We provide specialized training for our mentors who work with refugees so that we can nuture well-being and self-confidence as well as employability. Being proficient in English is currently a pre-requisite, but we are constantly working to broaden the languages of our mentor group in order to support a more diverse community of apprentices.

Launching a Pilot

Prosper has had the pleasure of partnering with Breaking Barriers and Makers Academy to pilot the Prosper Academy concept with a small cohort of refugees who are accepted asylum seekers in the UK (receiving housing, and job seekers allowance). The pilot is scheduled to launch early in 2016.

If you're a citizen or a refugee and are interested in growing your skills through contributing to refugee crisis projects, please fill out the join now form with a bit about yourself and what you'd like to join the program. We do have a bursary for refugees and are working with citizens to create custom skill plans that meet each apprentice's budget, dreams and lifestyle demands.
